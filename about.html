About No Such Furry Books

Independent publishing with heart

Our Story

No Such Furry Books is an independent children's book press dedicated to creating stories that matter. We believe that the best children's books don't just entertain—they help young readers understand themselves and the world around them.

In a world where children face increasingly complex emotional landscapes, we're committed to publishing books that naturally weave social-emotional learning into engaging, character-driven narratives. No lectures, no heavy-handed lessons—just authentic stories about real feelings, meaningful friendships, and the everyday adventures of growing up.

What We Believe

Emotional Intelligence Matters

We create stories that help children recognize, understand, and manage their emotions through relatable characters and situations. Social-emotional learning happens best when it's embedded naturally in compelling narratives.

Quality Over Quantity

Every book we publish meets professional standards for writing, illustration, and design. We invest in editing, professional illustration, and quality production to ensure our books compete with traditional publishers.

Diverse Voices & Experiences

Children deserve to see themselves in books and to encounter experiences different from their own. We're committed to authentic representation that reflects the real, diverse world kids live in.

Readers Come First

We create books that engage young readers while serving the adults who support them—parents, educators, and librarians looking for content that educates and entertains.

Our Approach

As an indie press, we have the freedom to take creative risks and focus on niche areas underserved by traditional publishing. We're particularly passionate about:

  • Social-Emotional Learning: Books that explore feelings, empathy, self-regulation, and relationship skills through storytelling rather than didactic lessons
  • Character-Driven Fiction: Stories where emotional intelligence and creative problem-solving emerge naturally through character actions and authentic situations
  • Authentic Representation: Diverse characters experiencing universal emotions and adventures, with cultural details woven naturally into stories
  • Age-Appropriate Content: Carefully crafted word counts, narrative structures, and illustration styles matched precisely to each age range

What's Next

We're currently developing our first titles across multiple age ranges—from picture books for ages 3-8 to middle grade novels for ages 8-12. Each book is being crafted with professional editing, quality illustration, and careful attention to both educational value and storytelling excellence.

Our Commitment to Quality

No Such Furry Books follows industry-standard practices for:

  • Professional editing and manuscript development
  • Licensed professional illustration with clear contracts
  • Accessible book design following WCAG guidelines
  • Proper metadata optimization for discoverability
  • Multi-format availability (print, ebook, audio when possible)
  • Library-quality production standards
